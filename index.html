Is Your Carpet Causing These Problems?

Dirty carpets are more than an eyesore — they're a real health and lifestyle problem. Here's what North Brisbane homeowners face every day:

Hidden Allergens & Bacteria

Dust, allergens, and bacteria stay trapped deep inside carpet fibres — vacuuming alone doesn’t fully remove them.

Pet Stains & Odours

Pet urine and odours soak deep into carpets, creating lingering smells and stubborn stains.

Unsafe for Kids

Kids spend more time close to the floor — dirty carpets may trigger allergies and irritation.

Embarrassing Carpet Appearance

Stains, smells, and dull carpets can make your home feel unclean when guests visit.

Expensive Replacement Costs

Replacing carpets can cost thousands — regular deep cleaning extends carpet life.

Rental Bond Problems

Dirty carpets are one of the top reasons tenants lose part of their rental bond.

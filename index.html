Is Your Carpet Causing These Problems?

Dirty carpets are more than an eyesore — they're a real health and lifestyle problem. Here's what North Brisbane homeowners face every day:

🤧

Hidden Allergens & Bacteria

Every square metre of carpet can hold up to 200,000 bacteria and years of dust mite waste. Regular vacuuming removes just 15% of what's actually lurking inside the fibres — the rest stays put.

🐾

Persistent Pet Stains & Odours

Pet urine and dander soak deep into the carpet backing and underlay. Store-bought sprays only mask the smell temporarily — and the stains reappear days later as if nothing happened.

👶

Unsafe for Your Kids

Children play, crawl, and breathe closest to the floor. A carpet full of chemicals, allergens, and pathogens can trigger asthma, eczema, and respiratory issues in little ones.

😓

Embarrassment When Guests Visit

Stained, dull, or smelly carpets can make even the most beautiful home feel unkempt. You shouldn't have to put rugs over problem areas just to feel comfortable having guests over.

💸

Early Carpet Replacement Costs

A new carpet installation in Queensland can cost $3,000–$8,000+. Regular professional cleaning extends your carpet's life by 5–10 years — saving you thousands in premature replacement.

🏠

Losing Your Bond on Rental

Landlords and property managers in North Brisbane are strict about carpet condition at end-of-lease inspections. Dirty carpets are the #1 reason renters lose part or all of their bond.
